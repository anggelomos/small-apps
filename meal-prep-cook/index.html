Simple steps

White rice
1
Rinse

Wash the rice 2-4 times, until the water is less cloudy.

2
Cook

Add rice, cold water, oil, and salt. Bring to a boil, cover, then lower the heat.

3
Check

Do not stir. It is ready when water is gone, small holes appear, and a grain tastes tender.

4
Rest

Turn off the heat. Keep covered for 5-10 minutes, then fluff and cool before storing.

For meal prep, let the rice cool before sealing containers. Fridge: 3-4 days. Freezer: several weeks.
